Service Overview

We assure you that our extensive range of parcel delivery services across Melbourne will be the perfect reason to choose us.

All your courier needs will be met with utmost efficiency, whatever the urgency or size of delivery. With a 24 hour courier service and a team of hard working professionals who work around the clock, we make sure that your deliveries are always on time.

As wide as our selection of services, the HR Logix network of courier vehicles also comprises, vans, UTEs, and semi-trailers. The fleet's vehicles are all meticulously maintained, and several have GPS tracking systems installed. Regardless of the time of day or night, all of our trucks and devoted workers always guarantee quick and secure delivery.

24 Hour Courier Service

From the initial point of contact to the safe delivery of your cargo, our 24 hour courier service staff provides great customer care. Our drivers are highly trained to transport every type of cargo safely, including time-sensitive and delicately handled items like medical, pathology, and blood-related products. We also have an immediate courier solution for emergencies.
