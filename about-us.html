THE TRUCK BRAND

We have a diverse national network of various Prime Movers and trailers, enabling us to match the ideal vehicle, or chain of vehicles, to suit your delivery and transportation needs. Nationally, our fleet network includes a wide range of trucks and trailers for large-scale linehaul and distribution solutions. Our Melbourne-based network includes everything from vans and Utes for handling smaller courier style deliveries up to trucks B Double-trailers for interstate &¬†local bulk freight solutions. All our vehicles undergo regular maintenance, safety and security checks to ensure ongoing efficient and reliable transport, no matter what you need delivered.

SAFETY AND COMPLIANCE

The safety of our drivers, other road users and the Australian community at large are very important to us. Driver fatigue affects much more than delivery times and we are committed to ensuring driver fatigue is not an issue, with a number of key measures and procedures in place to prevent it from occurring.
